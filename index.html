Instruction

The objective is to collect the most pairs of cards.

On each turn, a player turns over any two, three and four card (one at a time) and keeps them if the cards match (for example, two kings, three aces, four queen)

When a player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again

The trick is to remember which cards are where.

Maximum time is 60 seconds
